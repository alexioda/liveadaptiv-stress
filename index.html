Module 1: Belief & Longevity

We are taught to fear stress. Yet biology shows us that from humans to dogs, the absence of challenge leads to decay. It is not the load that breaks us; it is how we carry it.

üß¨ The Biology of Belief (1998 Study)

In a study of 30,000 adults over 8 years, researchers found that high stress increased the risk of dying by 43%, but only for those who believed stress was harmful. Those who experienced high stress but viewed it as a natural part of life had the lowest death rate.

Reflection: If you knew for a fact that this current challenge was actually strengthening your heart and extending your life, how would your attitude change right now?
